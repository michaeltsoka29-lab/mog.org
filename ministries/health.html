Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the church emphasize health so much?

We believe that our bodies are the temple of the Holy Spirit (1 Cor. 6:19). Physical and spiritual health are deeply connected. When our bodies are functioning at their best, our minds are clearer to understand God's word, and we have more energy to serve our community.

Do I have to be a vegetarian or vegan to attend?

Absolutely not! Everyone is welcome at Mothotlung SDA, regardless of their diet. While we advocate for a plant-based diet because of its proven health benefits and alignment with the original Eden diet, it is a personal choice and not a requirement for fellowship or salvation.

Are your health seminars and cooking classes free?

Yes, the majority of our community health programs, including our NEWSTART seminars and basic cooking classes, are completely free. Occasionally, there may be a small fee to cover the cost of special materials or ingredients, which will always be stated upfront.

What is the NEWSTART program?

NEWSTART is an acronym for eight simple, natural laws of health: Nutrition, Exercise, Water, Sunlight, Temperance, Air, Rest, and Trust in God. It is a globally recognized lifestyle program designed to prevent and reverse chronic diseases.

Common Questions

What should I wear?

You’ll see a mix of formal and casual. We value your presence more than your outfit!

Are children welcome?

Absolutely. We have dedicated classes for children to learn about Jesus in a fun way.

Is there a fellowship meal?

Yes! We often share a vegetarian lunch after the Divine Service. Please stay and eat with us.
